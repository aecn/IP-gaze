About Us

Gaze was founded in 2140 by our CEO Diana Percy. With New Earth now accessible to everyone thanks to international government distribution, Diana saw this transition as an opportunity for all people to start over. New Earth was a chance for a new life, and it held far more potential than the real world could ever offer. From her home, she worked tirelessly to create the first microchip and syringe that would soon be the passion project our company aims to improve every single day. With these two components, you are able to become who you were meant to be. You get to choose how people see you. You get to live the life you always wanted. Because with our help, life is limitless.
